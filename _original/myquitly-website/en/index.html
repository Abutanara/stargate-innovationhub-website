How the My Quitly quit smoking app works

My Quitly creates a personal reduction plan from your current smoking behavior and your desired quit date. The app determines how many cigarettes you smoke per day and how to reduce them step by step until you are completely smoke-free.

1

Start: Your current smoking behavior

At the beginning, you specify how many cigarettes you currently smoke, at what times you typically smoke, and when you want to quit. Based on this, My Quitly calculates your individual plan.

2

Personal reduction plan

Instead of stopping abruptly, you reduce in small, manageable steps. This way you avoid the shock of cold turkey and give your body and mind time to adjust.

3

Daily check-ins and tracking

You log every cigarette in the app. My Quitly shows you in real time whether you're on track, already over your limit today, or still have one "free".

4

Motivation & Gamification

With levels, streaks, badges and motivating messages, My Quitly keeps you engaged and supports you mentally during your quit smoking journey.

Frequently Asked Questions about the My Quitly Quit Smoking App

How does My Quitly differ from other quit smoking apps?

My Quitly works with a data-driven reduction plan instead of cold turkey and uses gamification for long-term motivation.
