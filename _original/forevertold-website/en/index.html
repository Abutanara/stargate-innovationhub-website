FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

Is ForeverTold really free?

Yes! The basic features are completely free: Unlimited story recording, automatic transcription and export as text. Premium features like AI-generated illustrations, voice cloning for audiobooks or special book designs are optionally available as in-app purchases.

How does the audio recording work?

It couldn't be simpler: Open a question, press the record button and tell your story. Our AI (OpenAI Whisper) automatically transcribes your words into text and cleans it up from filler words and slips. You can edit everything afterwards.

Who can see my stories?

Only you and the people you explicitly invite. Every story is private and protected by invitation codes. We don't sell data and don't show ads. Your memories belong to you.

Can I order a printed book?

You export a beautifully formatted PDF that you can have printed at any print service (e.g. Shutterfly, Mixbook, local print shop). We offer various layouts and premium templates with AI-generated illustrations.

Is the app suitable for older people?

Absolutely! ForeverTold was specifically designed for seniors and all generations. Large fonts, intuitive navigation, clear buttons and simple voice recording ‚Äì no technical knowledge needed. Many of our most active users are over 70.

What is the audiobook with voice clone?

A premium feature that clones the narrator's voice. You upload a 30-60 second recording, and our AI generates an audiobook that sounds like the real person. Perfect for preserving a loved one's voice forever.
