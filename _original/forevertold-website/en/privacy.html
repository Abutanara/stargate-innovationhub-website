Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

At ForeverTold, we take the protection of your personal data very seriously. This privacy policy informs you about how we collect, use and protect your personal data when you use our app or website.

Your memories and stories are sacred to us. We do not sell data and do not show ads.

2. Data Controller

The data controller is:

Stargate Innovation Hub
Germany
Email: info@stargate-innovationhub.com

3. Data We Collect

3.1 Registration Data

When registering, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Name (optional)

3.2 Usage Data

During app usage, the following data is processed:

  • Audio recordings of your stories
  • Transcribed texts
  • Project information (title, chapters)
  • Invitation codes for family members

3.3 Technical Data

  • Device information
  • IP address (anonymized)
  • App version

4. Purpose of Data Processing

We process your data exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Providing app functionality
  • Storage and synchronization of your stories
  • Transcription of audio recordings (via OpenAI Whisper)
  • Creation of audiobooks (via OpenAI TTS or FishAudio, only when used)
  • Generation of illustrations (via OpenAI DALL-E, only when used)
  • Technical support

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted services:

5.1 Supabase (Data Storage)

Your data is securely stored in the EU. Supabase is GDPR compliant.

5.2 OpenAI (Transcription & Images)

Audio recordings are transmitted to OpenAI for transcription. The data is not used for training.

5.3 FishAudio (Audiobook Voices & Voice Cloning)

Only when using the premium audiobook feature with voice cloning. Voice clones are stored encrypted.

5.4 OpenAI TTS (Standard Audiobooks)

For standard audiobooks we use OpenAI Text-to-Speech with 6 different voices.

6. Data Security

We protect your data through:

  • Encrypted transmission (TLS/SSL)
  • Encrypted storage
  • Access protection through authentication
  • Regular security audits

7. Your Rights

You have the right at any time to:

  • Access your stored data
  • Rectification of incorrect data
  • Deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Data portability (export your stories)
  • Object to processing

Contact us at info@stargate-innovationhub.com.

8. Data Retention

Your data is stored as long as your account is active. After account deletion, all data will be irrevocably deleted within 30 days.

9. Cookies

Our app does not use tracking cookies. On the website, we only use technically necessary cookies for functionality.

10. Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. For significant changes, you will be notified by email or in-app notification.

11. Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at:

Email: info@stargate-innovationhub.com
