FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most important questions about My Weddy.

Is My Weddy free?

Yes, My Weddy offers a free plan with the essential features like wedding website, guest list, and RSVP. For more features there are the Basic, Standard, and Pro plans – each a one-time payment, no subscription.

Can I use my own domain?

Yes, with the Pro plan you can use your own domain (e.g. anna-and-max.com) for your wedding website. All other plans come with a free My Weddy subdomain.

How does the RSVP system work?

Guests can accept or decline directly on your wedding website. You see all responses in real-time on your dashboard, including menu choices, allergies, and plus-one details.

Is my data safe with My Weddy?

Yes, My Weddy is GDPR compliant and stores all data securely on German/European servers. Your wedding website can additionally be protected with a password.

Can I use My Weddy on my phone?

Yes, My Weddy is a responsive webapp and works perfectly on all devices – desktop, tablet, and smartphone. No app installation required – just open in your browser.

Can multiple people plan together?

Yes! With the team feature, you can invite partners, best men, or family members. Depending on the plan, 1, up to 3, or unlimited team members are possible.

What happens to my data after the wedding?

Your data remains as long as you have an account. You can export all content at any time or request account deletion.
