FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most important questions about Weddy.

Is Weddy free?

Yes, Weddy offers a free Basic plan with all essential features like wedding website, guest list, and RSVP. For advanced features like budget planner, seating chart, and custom domain, there are Pro and Premium plans.

Can I use my own domain?

Yes, with the Premium plan you can use your own domain (e.g. anna-and-max.com) for your wedding website. Basic and Pro plans come with a free Weddy subdomain.

How does the RSVP system work?

Guests can accept or decline directly on your wedding website. You see all responses in real-time on your dashboard, including menu choices, allergies, and plus-one details.

Is my data safe with Weddy?

Yes, Weddy is GDPR compliant and stores all data securely on German/European servers. Your wedding website can additionally be protected with a password.

Can I use Weddy on my phone?

Yes, Weddy is a responsive webapp and works perfectly on all devices ‚Äì desktop, tablet, and smartphone. No app installation required ‚Äì just open in your browser.

Can multiple people plan together?

Yes! With the team feature, you can invite partners, best men, or family members. Depending on the plan, 1-3 or unlimited team members are possible.

What happens to my data after the wedding?

Your data remains as long as you have an account. You can export all content at any time or request account deletion.
