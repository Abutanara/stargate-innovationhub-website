FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most important questions about My Wishlists.

Is My Wishlists really free?

Yes, My Wishlists is completely free – for list owners and guests. There is no premium tier and no hidden fees. The platform is transparently funded through affiliate links to shops; the price never changes for you.

Do guests need an account to reserve wishes?

No. Guests simply open the list in their browser via the shared link and reserve with one click – no registration, no app. Reservations can also be cancelled if needed.

Can I see who reserved what from my wishlist?

By default, no – that's the surprise protection. Guests can see what's already taken (so nothing is bought twice), but as the list owner you never find out who picked what. Visibility can be adjusted per list.

How do I add wishes to my list?

Either manually or via product link: just paste the URL from any online shop and My Wishlists imports the title, image, description and price automatically. You can edit everything before saving – including quantity and priority per wish.

Can several people split a bigger gift?

Yes. Wishes can have a quantity (e.g. "3x") and guests can reserve just part of it – ideal for group gifts.

Is My Wishlists available in English?

The app is currently available in German. An English version is in preparation.

Is my data safe with My Wishlists?

Yes, My Wishlists is GDPR-compliant, uses no third-party tracking, and protects your lists with secret, unguessable links. You can delete your account and data yourself at any time.
