Frequently Asked Questions

Here you'll find answers to the most important questions.

Is ForeverTold free?

ForeverTold is free to use. For advanced features like audiobook generation and voice cloning, there are optional in-app purchases.

How does audio recording work?

Just press record and tell your story. Our AI converts your words into perfect text ‚Äî no filler words, natural flow.

Can I invite other people?

Yes! Share a code or QR code ‚Äî others can contribute or just read along. You keep full control over your story.

How does voice cloning work?

With a short audio recording (about 30 seconds), ForeverTold can clone the narrator's voice and generate the audiobook in that authentic voice.

Are my stories secure?

Yes, your stories belong to you. Secure storage, full control, no ads. Only you decide who has access.

Can my grandma/grandpa use the app?

Yes! ForeverTold is intentionally simple. Large fonts, clear buttons and audio recording instead of typing make it easy for all generations.

What languages is ForeverTold available in?

ForeverTold is currently available in German and English. More languages are planned.
