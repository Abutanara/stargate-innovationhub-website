Frequently Asked Questions About SmokeLess

Here you'll find answers to the most important questions about SmokeLess and quitting smoking.

Is SmokeLess really free?

SmokeLess is free to use. Optionally, you can unlock SmokeLess+ as a subscription (14-day free trial). Cancel anytime through your App Store settings.

How is SmokeLess different from other quit smoking apps?

SmokeLess uses a data-driven reduction plan instead of cold turkey and utilizes gamification for long-term motivation.

What happens if I smoke more than planned?

SmokeLess shows this transparently and can adjust the plan ‚Äì without moral judgments or blame.

Do I need an account for SmokeLess?

Yes, the app requires a (free) registration to save and sync your progress.

Yes, we keep your data secure and handle it according to the highest privacy standards in compliance with GDPR.

Can I use SmokeLess without internet?

Basic features are available offline. An internet connection is required for synchronization and updates.

How long does it take to become smoke-free with SmokeLess?

It depends on your individual plan. SmokeLess adapts to your pace ‚Äì from a few weeks to several months.
